SpaceForm is a UC Berkeley club focusing on innovating in-space manufacturing.






About

SpaceForm is a club that was established in 2022, with a goal of furthering the in-space manufacturing industry, an industry that will play a large role in our econamy over the next few years. Our goal is to help bulid the foundations for success for research to be done. We are currently working closely with different professors on campus in order to understand how we can have the most impact. Currently we are working on creating the worlds first database of materials in lunar conditions, which will lay the ground work for the next hundred years of space innovation.

Spring 2023 Project

First database for materials in lunar conditions

During our first semester, we are creating a tool that allows researchers and other professional in the Space and In Space Manufacturing to find materials that fit the situations that they need when creating technologies for in-space manufacturing.
