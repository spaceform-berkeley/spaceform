About

Established in 2022, SpaceForm is a student organization that aims to advance the frontiers of aerospace materials and in-space manufacturing, an industry that will play a large role in commercial space over the next decade. With a dedicated team of UC Berkeley undergraduates and cutting-edge projects at the forefront of materials science, computing, and engineering, our goal is to help build the foundations for success for space research. Spring 2023 Project

First database for materials in lunar conditions

Our goal is to create a material database for lunar conditions. This tool aids researchers and other professionals in the aerospace industry in better understanding material behavior in space environments and in selecting materials for technological innovation pertaining to in-space manufacturing.
